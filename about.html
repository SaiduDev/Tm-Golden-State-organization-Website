History of Golden State Organization

On the 16th October 2022, Two friends where discussing about a club that will connect the looking town community.
Mohamed lamin and saidu came up with the idea and they hosted their first meeting on that same faithfully day with five members, and they later grew to become one of the best clubs on the East,kissy to be specify.
Tm Golden State was a promising club over 21 members that were pleading just to be part of the community
They later moved from social club to an Organization, the Golden State Organization with the aim of helping the community and their country.
Due to rapid growth and improve ,G.S.O then have a young team YGSO, a sophisticated young guys that want to continue with the legacy of GSO.
As Golden State was rising, flying, and giving the true meaning of friendship, people were jealous and their jealousy then grewto to become enemies.

THE DOWNFALL OF G.S.O

On their first anniversary every GSO members and their fans were planning a blast and memorable anniversary and experience for all, but out of jealousy some fans who were admiring grow to become enemies.
On that historical day that GSO will always live to remember which is their 1th Anniversary a day that suppose to be a blessing and happiness for the Organization, but was ruined.
They manipulate on their food and nearly take the lifes of 12 members, people were crying and being pityful for the young guys who have done nothing to hurt peoples, but what they offer them was jealousy and enemity.
Praise be to God none was injured or died but its a historical moment for the Organization.
An Organization with over 17 members then become an Organization of less than 10 members, fans were asking about Golden state, but golden state busy building themselves before coming out to the fans.

THE GRADUAL GROWTH OF THE ORGANIZATION

Months and year had past, tm golden state was still in the shadow building and drowning in fears.
In 2025 GSO regained there strength again after electing two CEO's growth started.
Also members started coming again, but we lose our chair lady, she moved to another town, but that didn't stop us.
We then grew to be an Organization of 14 members,The down fall was never in our plan we were all hoping that the goods days will never last, forgetting to plan for the bad days.

LESSON LEARNED:

As a young Organization we taught the good old will never end, An Organization full with love was broken in a day.
Achievement and good things attract enemies
All of these heppened for a reason, but alhamudoulilah.
